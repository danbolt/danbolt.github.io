ABOUT

Hello! My name's Daniel, and I'm 23 years old! I currently live in the all-too-perfect city of Victoria, British Columbia, and recently graduated from the University of Victoria with a Bachelor's in Computer Science.

My interests include video games, programming languages, hiking, and cooking. I particularily enjoy drinking coffee on Saturday afternoons with my friends. My favourite book is The Brothers Karamazov by Fyodor Dostoyevsky.

Someday, I hope to have developed a bunch of fun video games, become a versatile cook, and maybe even finish something published by ATLUS. The third one is a bit of a long-shot though.

PROJECTS

Contract: Void Justice

Contract: Void Justice is a science fiction action game with procedurally-generated levels. The player steps into the shoes of a bounty hunter caught in a deep-space prison break.

Xbox

Zippy Push Kid

Zippy Push Kid is a cartoony platform game that's full of fun. Using the right analog stick, the player can propel themselves, push objects, and interact with enemies.

Xbox GitHub

Prince Protect

Prince Protect is a “boyfriend-defense” arcade game. One to two players attempt to keep their princes safe from ninjas as long as they can.

Download GitHub

Tumblr. The Game.

Tumblr. The Game. is a trivia challenge where you play as an alien attempting to study humanity through Tumblr. This is the brainchild of my good friend Rita, and I helped her with the design and implementation.
